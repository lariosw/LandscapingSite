Our School Day 9:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.

Your student will experience many opportunities for learning during his/her week at preschool. Each day is geared to a specific theme, letter and/or number. During the day your student will explore the theme through circle time, station time, academic work, art projects and other activities. The preschool program is specifically designed for the children in attendance each day. Teacher Barbara works with the younger than pre-k students. Students who are pre-k will spend class time with Teacher Nancy preparing themselves for kindergarten. Through the weekly theme and letter, your student will develop a foundation of skills and be excited and ready for their next educational experience.

"Lunch Bunch" Extended Day 12:00 p.m. - 1:00 p.m ( $5 fee/day)

This program add-on is designed for the student to have additional time and fun with their preschool friends. This time is for lunch (parent provided) as well as outdoor activities and enrichment time in a less formal, fun atmosphere.
