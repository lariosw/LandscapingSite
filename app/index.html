Welcome to Cleo Landscaping!

Cleo Landscaping is a Seattle area contractor who specializes in residential landscape and hardscape. We specialize in stunning landscaping and custom brick work. As leaders in the industry, we can help turn your property into the vision you always wanted.

About Us

Cleo Landscaping (lic#: CLEOLL*861DQ) is a dedicated team of landscape and hardscape experts with over 20 years experience. Our team is dedicated to design, installation and maintenance of residential properties in the surrounding Seattle area. From a small yard design to a large property rejuvenation, we will walk you through the journey of creating your own oasis. We focus on providing our clients with the highest quality material to work within any project's budget.
